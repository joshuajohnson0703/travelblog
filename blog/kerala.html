Kerala: A Family Connection

Kerala, a state located on the southwestern coast of India, holds a special place in my heart as my family is originally from there. Known for its beautiful beaches, backwaters, and lush green landscapes, Kerala is a popular tourist destination. The state is also famous for its delicious cuisine and traditional art forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam. Visiting Kerala with my family was a unique experience, as it allowed me to see the state through their eyes and learn more about our family's history and culture. We visited my grandparents' village and it was amazing to see how welcoming and friendly the local people were. I was particularly struck by the natural beauty of Kerala, from the lush green hills to the picturesque backwaters. The state is also known for its Ayurvedic treatments and I had the opportunity to experience one of the traditional massages and it was an incredible experience. Being able to share this experience with my family and explore our roots together made it even more special. Kerala is not only a tropical paradise but also a place that holds deep personal significance for me and my family. It will always hold a special place in my heart and I look forward to returning to this beautiful state again and again.
